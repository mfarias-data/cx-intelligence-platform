# Power BI Dashboard Design

## Executive Overview

### Purpose

Provide leadership with a high-level view of Customer Experience performance.

### KPIs

- Total Interactions
- Average CSAT
- Resolution Rate
- Reopen Rate
- Active Agents
- Average Handle Time

### Visuals

- KPI Cards
- Monthly Trend
- Channel Distribution

---

## Customer Experience Analysis

### Purpose

Understand customer satisfaction drivers.

### Visuals

- CSAT by Category
- CSAT by Channel
- Reopen Rate by Category
- Interaction Volume Trend

---

## Agent Performance

### Purpose

Evaluate operational efficiency.

### Visuals

- Agent Ranking
- Average Handle Time
- CSAT by Agent
- Reopen Rate by Agent

---

## Channel Performance

### Purpose

Compare channel effectiveness.

### Visuals

- Volume by Channel
- CSAT by Channel
- Reopen Rate by Channel

---

## Category Analysis

### Purpose

Identify major customer pain points.

### Visuals

- Volume by Category
- CSAT by Category
- Resolution Rate by Category
