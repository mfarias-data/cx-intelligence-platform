# Root Cause Analysis Framework

## Objective

Provide a structured approach for identifying operational issues and customer experience pain points.

---

## RCA Methodology

### Step 1 – Identify the Problem

Examples:

- Low customer satisfaction
- High reopen rate
- High average handle time

---

### Step 2 – Quantify the Impact

Questions:

- How many customers are affected?
- Which channel is impacted?
- Which category is impacted?

---

### Step 3 – Identify Patterns

Analyze:

- Channel trends
- Category trends
- Agent performance
- Time-based patterns

---

### Step 4 – Determine Possible Root Causes

Examples:

- Knowledge gaps
- Process inefficiencies
- Product issues
- System instability
- Staffing shortages

---

### Step 5 – Define Corrective Actions

Examples:

- Agent training
- Workflow redesign
- Process automation
- Product improvements
- Knowledge base updates

---

## Example Finding

### Problem

Billing category presents elevated reopen rates.

### Potential Root Causes

- Complex billing rules
- Lack of agent training
- Poor self-service experience

### Recommended Actions

- Billing workflow review
- Agent coaching sessions
- FAQ and self-service enhancement
